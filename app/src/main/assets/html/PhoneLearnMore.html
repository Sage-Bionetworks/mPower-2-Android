Your privacy is important to us

We ask for your phone number in order to verify if you have an account and sign you up in a secure manner.

The data which we collect is encrypted on your phone; meaning that your data is protected. Unauthorized people cannot easily understand your data when it is encrypted.

Instead of using your name or information that directly identifies you, we will use a code to label your study data. This code is random. It is not based on any of information that directly identifies you. Information about the code is stored in a separate database on a secure server. Only key people from our study team can link your identity to your study data.

We will combine your coded study data with the coded study data of other volunteers.

We will transfer this combined coded study data in the United States on the cloud-based Synapse data analysis platform.

WE WILL NEVER SELL, RENT, OR LEASE YOUR CONTACT INFORMATION.

Please read our Privacy Policy for more information.
